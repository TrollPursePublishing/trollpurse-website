History

Eight Hours

Eight Hours is the debut game of Troll Purse. Eight Hours is a survival horror game. Bill Norm - Paranormal Investigator - begins his investigation like any other, locked in by the owner. He must discover the source of the haunting over night. Little does he know, there are far more sinister entities that haunt this home. Eight Hours has been the development work of a single developer over the course of two years. It features the power of modern game engines and frightening real world EVP (Electronic Voice Phenomena).
